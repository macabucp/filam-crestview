Meet the Filipino-American Association of Crestview
The Filipino-American Association of Crestview is a vibrant community dedicated to bringing Filipinos together across the panhandle of Florida. We are passionate about fostering a sense of unity, celebrating our rich culture, and supporting each other through various initiatives and events.
Our Vision
We envision a thriving Filipino community where members can celebrate their heritage, share their experiences, and support each other's growth. Our vision is to be a cornerstone of cultural and social activities, promoting a strong sense of identity and belonging.
Our Values
  • Community: "We believe in the power of community and strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment."
  • Culture: "We celebrate our Filipino heritage and share it with pride."
  • Connection: "We foster connections between members, encouraging networking and collaboration."
  • Support: "We provide support to our members through various programs and initiatives.
    • Our Mission
    Our mission is to unite Filipinos in the region by providing a platform for social gatherings, cultural exchange, business networking, and community support. We aim to create an environment where members feel welcomed, connected, and empowered.
    Our History

    "Founded in 2010, the Filipino-American Association of Crestview has grown from a small group of friends to a large and diverse community. Over the years, we have hosted numerous events, launched various initiatives, and built lasting relationships. Our journey is a testament to the strength and resilience of our community."
